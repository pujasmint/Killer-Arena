Guidelines & Controls

  • Use arrow up & down to move your cute archer to align with arena demons
  • Use space for firing arrow. You can only fire one arrow at a time so aim right
  • Kill as many demons as possible in available time
  • All demons moves after respective times. Tough ones like "witch" move fast
  • If you kill a demon your score will be updated in score card based on demon difficulty
  • More powerful demons i.e Witch move really fast but if you kill them you get more points
  • Each demon kill will increase your available time also based on their difficulty
  • Your time will start with 30s but if you kill demons fast you can keep topping up your available time to even infinity!!
Trick: You can also kill more then one demon with one arrow if they are in line.
